FOOD ITEAMS

A samosa is a fried or baked pastry with a savory filling, most commonly a spiced mixture of potatoes and peas. The pastry is typically made with all-purpose flour (maida).

Biryani is a mixed rice dish popular in South Asia, typically made with rice, meat or vegetables, and a variety of spices. The key components include basmati rice, a protein source (often chicken, goat, or vegetables), yogurt, fried onions, and a blend of aromatic spices.

Typically, one pani puri consists of a hollow, fried wheat shell filled with a mixture of spiced mashed potatoes, chickpeas, tangy tamarind chutney, and a flavored water, often infused with mint and spices

A typical burger consists of a cooked patty, usually beef, placed between two halves of a bun. Common additions include lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, cheese, and various condiments like ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise.
